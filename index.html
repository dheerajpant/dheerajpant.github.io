Research experience : Grain Quality and Quantity Assessment

AI-Based Grain Quality and Quantity analyzer. Image Processing and Machine Learning techniques are at the core of the application. Users can classify various categories of grains, and visualize various statistics with our pre-trained models in very little time. Additional functionality like training your models is there, with a rollback option in case something goes wrong.

In this Research project, I am key participant in shape-based quality assessment. We designed and implemented fast algorithms for extracting features through color pixel values and various geometrical aspects in grain.
